Why No Conversions — Root Cause Analysis

Position + AI Overview Stack

Most commercial keywords sit position 8–20 nationally. AI Overviews appear above organic results, capturing intent before users scroll to RTM's listing.

Paid Competitor Ads

Competitors like WebFX and Hatch pay for top-of-page ads on the same commercial keywords RTM ranks for organically, taking clicks at every position.

Conversion Tracking Gap

GA4 is not measuring actual leads — no goal on /thank-you page. Organic conversions are likely higher than 2 but completely untracked.

Brand-Heavy Traffic

Of 364 clicks, ~135+ (37%) are brand queries. High engagement but low new-lead intent — these are people who already know RTM.

Internal Traffic Polluting GA4

~8,800+ sessions appear to be internal RTM/client traffic. Engagement rate and session data are distorted, making true organic performance hard to measure.

Mobile Experience Gap

Mobile engagement rate is 37.8% vs 54.2% desktop — a 16-point gap. Mobile users bounce more, possibly due to page speed or layout issues.
